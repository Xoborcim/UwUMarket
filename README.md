# 🏙️ PolyBot

A feature-rich, highly interactive Discord bot that transforms your server into a living town economy, complete with a co-op RPG dungeon crawler, an active job market, and a high-stakes casino.

## ✨ Core Features

### 🏛️ Living Town Simulation
The entire server works together (or against each other) to build and maintain a virtual town.
* **Live Town Board:** A dynamically generated, real-time emoji map that grows as the town levels up.
* **Supply & Demand:** Farmers must work to prevent server-wide **Famines** (which cut all paychecks by 50%).
* **Active Jobs:** Play interactive UI mini-games to earn your wage (e.g., push-your-luck mining, timed hacking puzzles).
* **Player-Driven Economy:** Miners gather ores, Blacksmiths forge them into RPG weapons, and players buy them on the P2P market.
* **Politics & Corruption:** Politicians set server-wide tax rates to fund town upgrades—or quietly embezzle the funds for themselves.

### ⚔️ Co-op Endless Dungeon (RPG)
Form a party of up to 3 players and dive into an endless, turn-based roguelike dungeon.
* **Classes & Builds:** Choose from 9 unique classes (Paladin, Cryomancer, Berserker, etc.), each with custom passives and scaling spell modifiers.
* **Deep Combat:** Features elemental weaknesses, tank taunts, boss "enrage" phases, and party-wide Relics.
* **Tiered Loot System:** Discover increasingly powerful weapons, armor, and spells the deeper you survive. Beware of Mimics!

### 🎰 The Casino
Risk your hard-earned wages in highly interactive, UI-driven gambling games.
* **Multiplayer Texas Hold'em:** Full poker engine supporting up to 6 players per table.
* **Advanced Blackjack:** Play against the dealer with support for Doubling Down and Splitting hands.
* **Mines:** A classic 20-tile push-your-luck grid game with dynamic multipliers.
* **Russian Roulette:** A high-stakes, winner-takes-all multiplayer survival game.
* **Unique Games:** Custom games like *Jad's Belly Slap*, Slots, and a Daily Server Lottery.
